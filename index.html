My Calorie Judge

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KruxLabs (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the My Calorie Judge mobile application (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: your email address and the name or nickname you provide. If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email and basic profile info.
  • Profile information you enter: birth date, sex, height, weight, activity level, goal, diet preference, and TDEE mode. These are used to calculate your calorie and nutrition targets.
  • Content you submit: photos of food, text descriptions of meals, and descriptions of physical activities.
  • Usage data: your food and activity logs (items, estimated calories/macros, timestamps) and daily counts of how many AI analyses you run (used to enforce free-tier limits).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the App’s features: authenticating you, calculating BMR/TDEE and nutrition targets, generating AI estimates, showing your dashboard and history, and enforcing usage limits. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. AI Processing

When you scan or describe a meal or activity, the relevant content (your photo or text, plus profile details like weight, height, age, and sex when needed for the estimate) is sent to our processing services — Google Cloud (Firebase) and the Google Gemini API — to generate the estimate. This processing happens on servers and is subject to Google Cloud’s data-protection terms.

4. Where Your Data Lives

Your account and logs are stored in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore), currently in the asia-southeast1 (Singapore) region. Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS), and access is restricted by security rules so only your authenticated account can read or write your data.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

If you purchase a subscription, payment is handled entirely by Google Play. We never see or store your card or payment details; we only receive confirmation of your subscription status.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at kruxlabs.apps@gmail.com; we will complete verified requests within 30 days.

7. Children

The App is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Contact us at kruxlabs.apps@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated in the App, and the “Last updated” date above will always reflect the current version.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: kruxlabs.apps@gmail.com
