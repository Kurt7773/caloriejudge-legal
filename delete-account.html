Delete Your Account

This page explains how to delete your My Calorie Judge account (operated by KruxLabs) and all of the data associated with it.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open My Calorie Judge and sign in.
  2. Open the menu and go to Edit Profile.
  3. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm by tapping Delete Forever.

Your account and all associated data are deleted immediately and permanently. This cannot be undone.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email us at kruxlabs.apps@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject "Delete my account." We will verify the request and complete it within 30 days.

What is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes, from our servers:

  • Your account and sign-in identity (email/Google account link, user ID).
  • Your profile (name, birth date, sex, height, weight, goals, and preferences).
  • All of your food logs and activity logs, including calories and macros.
  • Your history, daily summaries, and app usage counters.

Meal photos you scan are processed to generate an estimate and are not stored on our servers.

What is kept

After deletion we retain no personal data tied to your account. Anonymous, aggregated records that cannot identify you, or records we are legally required to keep, may be retained where applicable. Backups are purged on our standard rotation within 30 days.
